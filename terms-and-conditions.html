Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: May 5, 2026

By entering Nocta The Club or utilizing our digital services, you agree to adhere to the following Terms & Conditions. These regulations are designed to maintain the exclusive atmosphere and safety standards that define the Nocta experience.

1. Admission & Conduct

  • **Age Policy**: Admission is strictly reserved for individuals aged 18 and over. Valid government-issued photo identification is mandatory and will be verified upon entry.
  • **Entry Rights**: Management reserves the absolute right to refuse admission or eject any individual whose conduct is deemed incompatible with our venue's standards.
  • **Zero Tolerance**: Nocta maintains a zero-tolerance policy regarding illegal substances, harassment, or any form of aggressive behavior towards staff or fellow guests.

2. Dress Code Standard

To preserve our unique atmosphere, we enforce a strict **Elegant & Smart Casual** dress code. Nocta reserves the right to refuse entry to guests wearing:

  • Sportswear, tracksuits, or athletic-branded hoodies.
  • Inappropriate footwear including beach flip-flops or work boots.
  • Items of clothing deemed excessively distressed or casual for a premium nightlife setting.

3. Reservation Protocols

  • **Table Availability**: Table reservations are confirmed only upon receipt of an official confirmation message from our concierge team.
  • **Package Commitment**: Our Premium and VIP packages often involve a minimum spend commitment, which must be honored as part of the booking agreement.
  • **Arrival Policy**: Reserved tables are held for a 30-minute grace period. Beyond this time, the reservation may be released to accommodate other guests.

4. Media & Privacy

Entry to Nocta The Club constitutes consent to be featured in promotional photography or video content for our digital archives and social media platforms. If you wish to remain anonymous, please notify our floor manager upon your arrival.

5. Liability & Belongings

While we provide secure areas for personal belongings, Nocta The Club is not liable for the loss or damage of items brought into the venue. We encourage the use of our supervised cloakroom services.
