Privacy Policy

Last Revised: May 5, 2026

At **Nocta The Club**, we recognize that privacy is a critical aspect of the premium experience we provide to our guests. This Privacy Policy outlines our transparent approach to handling your personal data, ensuring that your journey through the Nocta experience—both digital and physical—remains secure and private.

1. Scope of Data Collection

In the pursuit of delivering an unparalleled clubbing experience in Baia Mare, we collect and process limited personal information necessary for our operations:

  • **Reservation Identity**: Full name, mobile contact, and email address provided during the booking process for table reservations and VIP packages.
  • **Preferences & Engagement**: Details regarding specific beverage choices or seating preferences to enhance your future visits.
  • **Digital Footprint**: Standard analytical data including IP addresses and interaction patterns with our digital archive and event voting systems.

2. Data Utilization

Your information is utilized solely to facilitate the high-standard service expected at Nocta:

  • **Operational Efficiency**: Confirming and managing table allocations, VIP entry protocols, and group attendance.
  • **Personalized Communication**: Providing timely updates about your reservation or informing you of changes to our upcoming sequences.
  • **Exclusive Invitations**: Periodically sharing news about premier events or private sessions with guests who have opted into our inner circle.

3. Security Protocols

Nocta The Club employs advanced technical safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access. Our internal protocols ensure that personal information is handled with the same level of care as our most exclusive VIP suites.

4. Third-Party Collaboration

We do not share your data for commercial gain. We collaborate only with essential service partners (e.g., our reservation platform and Google location services) who adhere to strict data protection standards.

5. Institutional Compliance

We comply with all national and international data protection regulations, including GDPR. You maintain the right to request a copy of your data, its correction, or its complete erasure from our archives.
